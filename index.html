Palindrome or nothing special?

How to spot a palindrome!

Palindromes are words or numbers (or a combination) that are the same when you read them backwards as when you read them forwards.

For this checker we will ignore any whitespaces, characters that aren't alphanumeric as well as if the word or phrase is upper or lower case.

To help you think of some possiblities to check, here are some examples of palindromes:

  • 121
  • refer
  • 356653
  • race car
  • pot6jk4kj6top
